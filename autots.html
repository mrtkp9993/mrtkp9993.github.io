AutoTS - One click univariate time series modeling and forecasting tool

AutoTS is a powerful tool designed to make time series modeling and forecasting as easy as possible. With just one click, you can use state-of-the-art statistical and machine learning algorithms to model and forecast your univariate time series data. Whether you're a data scientist or a business analyst, AutoTS can help you quickly and easily make predictions about your data.

Note: This software is closed source and can be accessed via a web browser or API. Please contact for licensing and pricing information.
