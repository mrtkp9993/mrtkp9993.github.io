AutoAD - One click anomaly detection tool - Why anomaly detection is important?

Anomaly detection is the process of identifying patterns that deviate from what is considered to be typical or anticipated behavior. Anomalous events can cause businesses to lose millions of dollars. Millions of dollars can likewise be lost by consumers. In addition, there are several circumstances every day where people's lives and property are in danger. It's a concern if your bank account is emptied. It's an issue if your water pipe bursts and floods your basement. There will be a problem if all flights are delayed.

Example use cases

Telecommunication: Detect Roaming abuse, Revenue fraud, Service disruptions
Banking: Flag Abnormally high transactions, Fraudulent activity, Phishing
Environmental: Detect Deforestation, Melting of glaciers, Air / water quality changes
Healthcare: Prevent fraud, Detect illnesses, Implement preventative policies
Transportation: Detect abnormal traffic patterns
Social Media: Detect Hacked accounts, Spamming, False ads, Fake news
Finance and Insurance: Detect fraudulent claims / transactions / expenses
Cybersecurity: Detect anomalous activity
Surveillance: Detect anomalous activity (intruder walking on your lawn, etc.) from video cameras
Manufacturing: Detect malfunctions or failure of manufacturing system
Retail: Detect inefficiency of any process and improve them
