Return Policy

Thank you for purchasing our app! We want you to have the best experience possible. If you encounter any issues or are dissatisfied with your purchase, please review our return policy below.

1. Google Play Store Purchases

For apps purchased through the Google Play Store:

  • Refund Window: You can request a refund within 48 hours of purchase. After this period, refunds are managed by Google and are subject to their policies.
  • To request a refund:
    • Open the Google Play Store app.
    • Tap your profile icon > Payments & subscriptions > Budget & history.
    • Find the order you want to return and select Refund.
    • Follow the on-screen instructions.
  • If you encounter any problems, please contact Google Play support directly here.

  • Apple handles all refunds and disputes for App Store purchases. You can contact Apple Support here.

3. Exceptions

  • Refunds may not be issued for issues related to non-compatibility or device-specific performance problems that do not stem from the app itself.
  • Refunds will not be granted for services or content that have been fully used or consumed after purchase.
